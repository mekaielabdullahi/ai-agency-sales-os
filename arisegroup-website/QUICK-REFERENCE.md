## Design Rules

### Colors
- Primary: Navy (#1a1f36)
- Accent: Electric Blue (#00A5E3)
- Background: White (#FFFFFF)
- Text: Dark Gray (#2C3E50)

### Typography
- Font: Inter or Poppins
- H1: 48-56px (desktop)
- Body: 16-18px
- Line height: 1.6-1.8

### Spacing
- Section padding: 80-100px (desktop)
- Card padding: 32-40px
- Element spacing: 24-32px

### Buttons
- Primary: Accent background, white text
- Secondary: Outline style
- Padding: 16px × 32px
- Radius: 4-8px

---

## Responsive Breakpoints

- **Desktop:** 1440px+ (4-col grids)
- **Laptop:** 1024-1439px (maintain multi-col)
- **Tablet:** 768-1023px (2-col grids)
- **Mobile:** <768px (single column stack)

## Do's and Don'ts

### DO
✅ Enterprise, minimal, modern aesthetic
✅ Clear CTAs above the fold
✅ Trust signals throughout
✅ Mobile-first responsive design
✅ Scannable content (bullets, short paragraphs)

### DON'T
❌ Sci-fi or cluttered AI imagery
❌ Make it look like SaaS self-serve
❌ Copy competitor layouts
❌ Hide CTAs or make them hard to find
❌ Use technical jargon in hero/main sections
