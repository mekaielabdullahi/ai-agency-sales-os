## DM Playbook — For All 6 Posts

### Tier 1 — Hot Engagement (detailed comment, shares their pain, asks a question)
**Send within 2 hours.**

> "Hey [Name] — thanks for the comment. What you described sounds exactly like what we deal with in the first 30 minutes of every discovery call.
>
> Would you be open to a quick 20-minute conversation? No pitch — just want to understand the situation better and share what we've seen work for businesses like yours."

---

### Tier 2 — Warm Engagement (likes + short comment, or just a like from someone in ICP)
**Send within 24 hours.**

> "Hey [Name] — noticed you engaged with the post. Curious what resonated?
>
> I work with service business owners in the $1M-$10M range who are tired of doing everything manually. If that sounds familiar, I'd love to connect."

---

### Tier 3 — Profile Visit (viewed your profile after post)
**If Unipile shows profile views — send within 48 hours.**

> "Hey [Name] — saw you stopped by the profile. I'm Mekaiel, founder at Arise Group. We help service businesses automate operations and free up their time.
>
> Would love to connect if what I'm building is relevant to what you're working on."

---

### Discovery Call DM (after warm exchange — 2nd or 3rd message)

> "Given everything you've shared — would you be open to a 30-minute call this week? I can walk you through exactly what a $5K AI sprint would look like for a business like yours and whether the ROI math makes sense.
>
> No pressure. Worst case you walk away with a clear picture of what's possible."
