# Welcome Email (Handoff)

**Stage:** 5 - Handoff
**Touchpoint:** #20
**Type:** Email
**Automation:** Auto-send (triggered by payment confirmation)

---

## Purpose

Welcome new implementation client and introduce them to the delivery team.

---

## Template

```
Subject: Welcome to AriseGroup! Meet Your Delivery Team

Hey [First Name],

Congratulations - we're officially kicking off your project!

**Quick Intro:**

I'm handing you off to our delivery team who will take amazing care of you from here.

Your main contacts:
- **[Delivery Lead Name]** - Your project lead (cc'd)
- **[Support Contact]** - For any questions

**What Happens Next:**

1. You'll receive a **Welcome Packet** with everything you need to know
2. We'll send an **Onboarding Form** to collect access credentials
3. Your **Kickoff Call** will be scheduled within [timeframe]

**My Role Going Forward:**

I'll still be around! Feel free to reach out if you ever need anything. I'll also check in periodically to make sure everything's going smoothly.

Excited to see what we build together!

[Sales Rep Signature]

---

P.S. [Delivery Lead Name] will be reaching out within 24 hours to get things rolling.
```

---

## Trigger

- Contract signed AND payment confirmed

---

## Automation Setup

1. Triggered automatically when deal marked "Closed Won" in CRM
2. CC delivery lead on the email
3. Create handoff task for delivery team
4. Move client to "Active" status

---

## Notes

- This bridges sales to delivery - make it warm
- Delivery team should follow up within 24 hours
- Sales stays in the loop but steps back
