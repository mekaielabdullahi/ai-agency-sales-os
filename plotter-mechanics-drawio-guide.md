## GENERAL DRAW.IO TIPS

### Color Palette:
- **Green**: Revenue, opportunity, positive outcomes (#4CAF50)
- **Blue**: Operations, technology, systems (#2196F3)
- **Red**: Pain points, critical issues (#F44336)
- **Yellow**: Medium priority, warnings (#FFC107)
- **Orange**: Customers, engagement (#FF9800)
- **Gray**: Support functions, backend (#9E9E9E)
- **Purple**: Strategic initiatives (#9C27B0)

### Typography:
- **Titles**: 18-24pt, Bold
- **Subtitles**: 14-16pt, Semi-bold
- **Body Text**: 10-12pt, Regular
- **Labels**: 8-10pt, Regular

### Shapes:
- **Processes**: Rounded rectangles
- **Decisions**: Diamonds
- **Data/Inputs**: Parallelograms
- **Start/End**: Ovals
- **Groupings**: Containers with light background

### Connectors:
- **Data flow**: Solid arrows
- **Process flow**: Solid arrows
- **Dependencies**: Dashed arrows
- **Relationships**: Lines without arrows
