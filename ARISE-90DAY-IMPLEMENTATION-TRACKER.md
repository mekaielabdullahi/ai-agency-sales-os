## BLOCKER RESOLUTION PROTOCOL

**When you hit a blocker:**

1. **Document it:**
   - What's the blocker?
   - Why is it blocking you?
   - What have you tried?

2. **Attempt self-resolution:**
   - Can you solve it yourself?
   - Who can help?
   - What resources do you need?

3. **Escalate if needed:**
   - Flag in weekly stand-up
   - Request leadership support
   - Get decision/resources

4. **Resolve & document:**
   - How was it resolved?
   - How to prevent next time?
   - Update processes
