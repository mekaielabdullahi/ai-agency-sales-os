## Writing Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- 40-50 characters ideal
- Create curiosity without clickbait
- Avoid spam trigger words (FREE, !!!, ACT NOW)
- Test questions vs statements
- Personalize when possible

### Pre-Header Text
- 85-100 characters
- Expands on subject line
- Creates more curiosity
- Don't repeat subject exactly

### Email Body
- Conversational tone (write like you talk)
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Use whitespace generously
- One main idea per email
- One CTA per email (not multiple asks)

### CTAs
- Button > Link > Text
- Action-oriented text ("Get My Free Audit" > "Click Here")
- Repeat CTA if email is long
- Make benefit clear

---

## Sending Best Practices

### Frequency
- Weekly minimum for nurture
- Max 3x/week for most audiences
- Consistency > frequency

### Send Times
- B2B: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 1-3 PM
- Test your specific audience
- Avoid Mondays (inbox overload) and Fridays (checked out)

### List Management
- Segment by engagement
- Re-engagement campaigns for inactive
- Easy unsubscribe (retain trust)
