# Outreach Best Practices

**Source**: Week 3 Execution + Client Outreach Methodology

---

## Quality Over Quantity

**Do**:
- ✅ 10 perfect messages > 100 generic ones
- ✅ 10-12 min per message (research + personalization)
- ✅ Specific observation about them in first line
- ✅ Under 100 words (LinkedIn)

**Don't**:
- ❌ Copy-paste same message
- ❌ Generic "Dear Sir/Madam"
- ❌ Lead with pitch
- ❌ Wall of text (300+ words)

---

## Timing & Frequency

**Best Days**: Tuesday-Thursday
**Best Times**: 9-11 AM, 2-4 PM (local time)
**Follow-up**: Day 1, Day 3, Day 7

---

## Response Management

**Speed Matters**:
- Hot leads: Within 1 hour
- Warm leads: Within 4 hours
- All others: Within 24 hours

**Always**:
- Schedule next step before ending conversation
- Send calendar invite immediately
- Thank them for their time

---

## Success Metrics

- Response rate: 15-20% is excellent
- Discovery call show rate: 50%+ is good
- Quality > quantity always
