# Discovery Call Best Practices

**Source**: Discovery Call Methodology

---

## Preparation

**15 min before call**:
- Review their LinkedIn thoroughly
- Check company website
- Identify 2-3 likely pain points
- Prepare specific questions
- Have note template ready

---

## During Call

**The 70/30 Rule**: They talk 70%, you talk 30%

**Structure**:
- Minutes 0-5: Rapport & context
- Minutes 5-20: Discovery questions
- Minutes 20-28: Value mapping
- Minutes 28-30: Next steps

**Questions That Work**:
- "Walk me through a typical day/week"
- "What's your biggest operational friction point?"
- "If you could automate one thing, what would it be?"

---

## After Call

**Within 4 hours**:
- Send thank-you email
- Send calendar invite for next step
- Clean up notes
- Add to tracking

**Within 24 hours**:
- Send promised resources
- Begin audit creation if committed

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

- ❌ Talking too much (pitch mode)
- ❌ Not taking notes
- ❌ Going over time
- ❌ Vague next steps
- ❌ Forgetting to listen
