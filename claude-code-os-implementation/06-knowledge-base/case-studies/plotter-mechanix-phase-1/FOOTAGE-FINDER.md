## How to Access Fireflies

Fireflies is where we save video recordings of our meetings.

### Step 1: Log In

1. Go to [app.fireflies.ai](https://app.fireflies.ai)
2. Log in with AriseGroup credentials (ask Mekaiel if you need access)

### Step 2: Find a Meeting

1. Click "Meetings" in the left menu
2. Use the search bar at the top
3. Type the meeting name (see Meeting Index below)

### Step 3: Watch and Find Timestamps

1. Click on a meeting to open it
2. Use the video player to watch
3. Look at the transcript on the right side
4. Timestamps are shown next to each speaker
5. Use Ctrl+F (or Cmd+F on Mac) to search for words

### Step 4: Download a Clip

**Full video:** Click the download icon (arrow pointing down)

**Short clip:**
1. Note the start time and end time
2. Ask Mekaiel to export that section
3. OR use video editing software to cut it yourself
