## The Characters

### Main Character: Kelsey
- **Who is he:** Owner of Plotter Mechanix (printer repair company)
- **Location:** Phoenix, Arizona, USA
- **Age:** Middle-aged man
- **Personality:** Talks a lot, energetic, sometimes frustrated
- **His dream:** Start a ministry to help young people without fathers

### Supporting Character: Alyssa
- **Who is she:** Office manager
- **Where she works:** From home
- **Her job:** Handle customer calls, schedule jobs, do paperwork
- **Her problem:** Too many emails, too much to track

### Supporting Character: Joe
- **Who is he:** New technician (employee)
- **His story:** Learning to do repairs by himself
- **His success:** Making money on his own now

### Supporting Character: Megan
- **Who is she:** Outside consultant (expert)
- **Her role:** Gives professional opinion about Kelsey's problems

---

## The Story (Beginning, Middle, End)

### BEGINNING (The Problem)
Kelsey's business is chaos:
- He works 70+ hours a week
- His phone never stops ringing
- Information gets lost between him and Alyssa
- He cannot take a day off
- He tried technology before and it failed

### MIDDLE (The Solution)
AriseGroup comes to help:
- We listen and understand his problems
- We find the biggest problem to fix first
- We build a phone system that records calls
- Calls automatically create tasks for Alyssa

### END (The Results)
Everything is better:
- Information stops getting lost
- Alyssa gets everything automatically
- Kelsey trusts the system
- He can see how to grow the business now
