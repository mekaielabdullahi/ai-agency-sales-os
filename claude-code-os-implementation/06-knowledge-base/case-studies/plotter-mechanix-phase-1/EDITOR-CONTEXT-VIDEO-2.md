## Who is AriseGroup?

AriseGroup is a small company with 4 partners.
We help businesses work better using technology and AI.

**What makes us different:**
- We understand the business FIRST
- Then we build the right solution
- We stay with clients long-term
- We only build things that make money back

---

Each partner has a different job:

### Chris Andrade
- **His job:** Builds relationships, finds new clients
- **He says:** "I'm Chris. I build relationships and open doors."
- **Simple version:** He is the salesman and connector

### Trent Christopher
- **His job:** Designs the technical systems
- **He says:** "I'm Trent. I architect the technical systems."
- **Simple version:** He is the technical planner

### Matthew Kerns
- **His job:** Builds and tests everything
- **He says:** "I'm Matthew. I make sure what we build actually works."
- **Simple version:** He is the engineer who builds things

### Mekaiel Abdullahi
- **His job:** Manages projects, translates business to technical
- **He says:** "I'm Mekaiel. I translate business needs into builds."

## What This Video Should Feel Like

**Mood:** Professional but friendly

**Think of it like:** Meeting a team of experts
- Trustworthy
- Smart but not showing off
- Friendly, approachable

**Music:** Soft, inspiring background music
- Not too loud (voice must be clear)
- Professional but not boring
- Gets a little louder during transitions
