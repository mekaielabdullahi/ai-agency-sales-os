## Animation Specifications

**General:**
- Smooth transitions (0.5-1s)
- Consistent easing curves
- Professional but not overly flashy

**Number Counters:**
- Duration: 2-3 seconds
- Easing: Ease-out for dramatic effect
- Final number emphasis (scale up briefly)

**Diagram Builds:**
- Step-by-step reveal
- Arrows animate in sequence
- Icons fade/scale in

---

## Delivery Format

- **Graphics:** PNG with transparency where needed
- **Animations:** After Effects project files or exported video
- **Lower thirds:** Transparent background video files
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160) preferred, 1080p minimum
- **Frame rate:** Match video (24fps or 30fps)
