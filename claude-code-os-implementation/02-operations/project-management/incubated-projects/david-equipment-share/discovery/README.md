# Discovery Documentation - David Equipment Share

## Overview
This folder contains all discovery materials from the initial analysis of David Equipment Share's CRM requirements for the construction equipment rental and sales market.

## Discovery Timeline

### December 12, 2025 - Initial Discovery Call
- **Participants:** David, Chris, Development Team
- **Purpose:** Requirements gathering for custom CRM
- **Outcome:** Partnership model agreed, MVP in 1-3 weeks

## Key Findings

### The Core Problem
No integrated CRM solution for construction equipment rental/sales sector, leading to:
- Lost sales from manual follow-ups
- No phone/CRM contact sync
- Lack of equipment-specific pricing
- Poor team task coordination

### Business Impact
- Manual processes losing 20-30 leads/month
- 10+ hours/week wasted on admin
- No visibility into sales pipeline
- Team coordination breakdown

### Solution Path
Custom CRM with partnership model:
- No upfront investment required
- Co-ownership of IP
- MVP in 1-3 weeks
- Potential resale to Equipment Share
