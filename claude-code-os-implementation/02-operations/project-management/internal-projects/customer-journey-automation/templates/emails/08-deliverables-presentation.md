# Deliverables Presentation Email

**Stage:** 3 - AI Audit
**Touchpoint:** #15
**Type:** Email
**Automation:** Template (human sends with deliverables)

---

## Purpose

Deliver audit results and schedule presentation call.

---

## Template

```
Subject: Your AI Audit Results Are Ready

Hey [First Name],

Your AI Audit is complete! I've attached the full deliverables package:

**What's Included:**

1. **Business Functions Map**
   - Your 10 core functions mapped
   - Ownership and gaps identified
   - Risk levels assessed

2. **Opportunity Matrix**
   - [X] automation opportunities identified
   - Prioritized by impact and effort
   - ROI estimates based on the numbers you shared

3. **PRD + Roadmap + Money Slide**
   - Detailed implementation plan
   - Phased approach with milestones
   - Investment breakdown

**Next Step:**

Let's schedule a 30-minute call to walk through everything and answer questions. I want to make sure you get full value from this.

Available times:
- [Option 1]
- [Option 2]
- [Option 3]

Looking forward to presenting these findings!

[Signature]

---
**Attachments:**
- [Company]_Business_Functions_Map.pdf
- [Company]_Opportunity_Matrix.pdf
- [Company]_PRD_Roadmap.pdf
```

---

## Checklist Before Sending

- [ ] All three deliverables completed and reviewed
- [ ] Documents branded and formatted
- [ ] ROI numbers verified (no fabricated data)
- [ ] Presentation call times available
- [ ] Files attached

---

## Notes

- Always present live - don't just send and hope
- The presentation is where you transition to the implementation proposal
- Be ready to answer questions on investment
