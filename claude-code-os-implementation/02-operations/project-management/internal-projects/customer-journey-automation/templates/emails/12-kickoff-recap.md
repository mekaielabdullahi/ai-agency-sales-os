# Kickoff Recap Email

**Stage:** 6 - Onboarding
**Touchpoint:** #24
**Type:** Email
**Automation:** Template (human sends after kickoff call)

---

## Purpose

Summarize kickoff call outcomes, confirm scope, and list action items.

---

---

## Checklist Before Sending

- [ ] All scope items accurately listed
- [ ] Timeline dates realistic and confirmed
- [ ] Action items have clear owners and due dates
- [ ] Next milestone date set

---

## Notes

- Send within 2 hours of kickoff ending
- Be specific - vague recaps cause scope creep
- This becomes the reference document for the project
