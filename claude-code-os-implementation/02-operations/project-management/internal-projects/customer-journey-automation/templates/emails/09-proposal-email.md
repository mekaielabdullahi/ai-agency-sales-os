# Proposal Email

**Stage:** 4 - Implementation Proposal
**Touchpoint:** #16
**Type:** Email
**Automation:** Template (human sends after presentation)

---

## Purpose

Send formal implementation proposal based on audit findings.

---

## Template

```
Subject: Implementation Proposal for [Company Name]

Hey [First Name],

As promised, here's the implementation proposal based on your AI Audit findings.

**Quick Recap:**

During our audit, we identified [X] opportunities that could:
- [Key outcome 1 - use their numbers]
- [Key outcome 2 - use their numbers]
- [Key outcome 3 - use their numbers]

**Proposed Solution:**

Based on what we found, I'm recommending we start with:

1. **[Automation 1]** - [Brief description, expected outcome]
2. **[Automation 2]** - [Brief description, expected outcome]
3. **[Automation 3]** - [Brief description, expected outcome]

**Investment:**

[Package name]: $[Amount]

This includes:
- [Deliverable 1]
- [Deliverable 2]
- [Deliverable 3]
- [X] days/weeks of support post-launch

**Timeline:** [X weeks] from kickoff to go-live

**Next Steps:**

If you're ready to move forward:
1. Sign the attached agreement
2. Submit first payment
3. We schedule your kickoff call

**Questions?**

Happy to jump on a call to walk through anything. I have time [Day] at [Time] if that works.

[Signature]

---
**Attachments:**
- [Company]_Implementation_Proposal.pdf
- [Company]_Service_Agreement.pdf
```

---

## Personalization Checklist

- [ ] Reference specific audit findings
- [ ] Use their ROI numbers (not generic)
- [ ] Align deliverables to their priorities
- [ ] Include realistic timeline

---

## Notes

- Send within 48 hours of deliverables presentation
- Always attach the formal proposal document
- Include clear next steps with contract
