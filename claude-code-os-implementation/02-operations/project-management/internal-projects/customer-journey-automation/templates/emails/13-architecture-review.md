# Architecture Review Email

**Stage:** 7 - Build
**Touchpoint:** #25
**Type:** Email
**Automation:** Template (human sends with design docs)

---

## Purpose

Present technical architecture for client approval before building.

---

## Template

```
Subject: Architecture Review - Ready for Your Approval

Hey [First Name],

We've completed the technical design for your project. Before we start building, I want to make sure you're aligned on the approach.

---

**WHAT WE'RE BUILDING**

[Brief 2-3 sentence summary of the solution]

---

**ATTACHED DOCUMENTS**

1. **System Architecture** - How everything connects
2. **Data Flow Diagram** - What moves where
3. **Integration Map** - Systems involved

---

**KEY DECISIONS FOR YOUR APPROVAL**

1. **[Decision 1]**
   - Our recommendation: [X]
   - Why: [Brief reason]

2. **[Decision 2]**
   - Our recommendation: [X]
   - Why: [Brief reason]

3. **[Decision 3]**
   - Our recommendation: [X]
   - Why: [Brief reason]

---

**NEXT STEPS**

1. Review the attached documents
2. Let me know if you have questions
3. Reply with your approval (or let's schedule a call)

Once approved, we move into the build phase.

---

**TIMELINE IMPACT**

Approval needed by [Date] to stay on track for [Go-Live Date].

---

Questions? Happy to jump on a call to walk through anything.

[Signature]

---
**Attachments:**
- [Project]_System_Architecture.pdf
- [Project]_Data_Flow.pdf
- [Project]_Integration_Map.pdf
```

---

## Checklist Before Sending

- [ ] Architecture documents complete and reviewed
- [ ] Key decisions clearly stated
- [ ] Approval deadline included
- [ ] Files attached and named properly

---

## Notes

- Get explicit approval before building (email confirmation minimum)
- If they have questions, schedule a call - don't just email back and forth
- Document any scope changes requested at this stage
