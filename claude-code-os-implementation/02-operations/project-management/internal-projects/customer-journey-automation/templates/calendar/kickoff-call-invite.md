## Implementation Notes

**Platform Options:**
- Calendly
- Cal.com
- Google Calendar direct
- HubSpot Meetings

**Integrations:**
- Zoom (meeting link)
- CRM (log meeting)
- Project management (create kickoff task)
- Email (confirmation + reminder)

**Automation Flow:**
1. Onboarding form completed
2. Check delivery team availability
3. Propose kickoff times to client
4. Client confirms time
5. Calendar invite sent to all attendees
6. Reminder sent 24hr before
7. Meeting occurs
8. Kickoff recap triggered
