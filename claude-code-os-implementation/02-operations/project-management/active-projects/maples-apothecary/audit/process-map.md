## Business Overview

Maples Apothecary is a specialty plant and exotic goods retail shop located in Oldtown Yuma, AZ. The business focuses on rare imported plants, taxidermied insects, and plant education classes. Toby (owner) and his wife Susie operate the business with minimal staff support.

**Key Details:**
- Location: Oldtown Yuma (revitalizing downtown district)
- Recently expanded into larger front-facing storefront
- Operating hours: 28 hours/week (Tues-Sat 11-5, Sun 12-4)
- Current staff: Toby + Susie + 1 part-time helper (Fridays, $10/hr)
- Primary tools: Square POS, Instagram, ChatGPT for marketing

## Process Efficiency Analysis

### Time Waste Identification

| Process Area | Issue | Impact | Addressable? |
|-------------|-------|--------|--------------|
| **Customer Acquisition** | No systematic marketing | Primary revenue bottleneck | ✅ YES - Marketing automation |
| **Inventory Entry** | Forgetting to log arrivals | 10 hrs/month fixing, $750 value | ✅ YES - Process/checklist |
| **Daily Operations** | No SOPs or checklists | Can't delegate, owner-dependent | ✅ YES - Documentation |
| **Marketing Content** | Ad-hoc, no schedule | Inconsistent presence | ✅ YES - Playbook + delegation |
| **Grow Operations** | Manual watering/monitoring | Time-intensive, error-prone | ✅ YES - Automation ($5k) |
| **Accounting** | QuickBooks behind | Tax stress, no visibility | ⚠️ Friend helping |
