### Capability 1: Meta Pixel Tracking

**Finally see who's coming from your ads.**

We install Meta's tracking pixel on your lead capture page. When someone signs up after clicking an ad, you'll know exactly which ad brought them in.

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Blind spend | Full visibility |
| "Did that ad work?" | "That ad brought 12 leads this week" |

---

### Capability 2: Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Know exactly who buys from you.**

We combine your captured leads with 3 years of sales history to build a profile of your ideal customer:

- Demographics (age, location, household)
- Shed preferences (size, style, price range)
- Geographic patterns (which markets convert best)
- Buying triggers (what makes them say yes)

---

### Capability 3: Targeted Audiences (Lookalikes)

**Find more customers like your best buyers.**

Once we know who your customers are, Meta can find thousands of similar people. Instead of "shotgunning" to everyone, you target people who look like the ones already buying.

| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| Show ads to everyone | Show ads to people like your buyers |
| Hope for the best | Precision targeting |

---

### Capability 4: CPL/CAC/ROAS Tracking

**Know what every lead costs.**

With proper tracking, you'll finally answer: "Which ads actually drive sales?"

| Metric | What It Tells You |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Cost per Lead (CPL)** | How much you pay for each sign-up |
| **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | Total cost to get a sale |
| **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)** | Dollars back for every dollar spent |

---

### Capability 5: Seasonal Campaign Data

**Advertise the right sheds at the right time.**

Your captured data reveals patterns: which sheds sell in spring vs. fall, which sizes move fastest, when buyers are most active.

| Insight | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Cabins peak in March-April | Push cabin ads in February |
| Lofted barns sell year-round | Steady budget allocation |
| Weekends drive 60% of visits | Increase weekend ad spend |
