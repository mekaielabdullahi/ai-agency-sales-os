## Statement of Work

---

### 1. Project Overview

Contractor will establish the operational foundation for Client's business, including validated baseline metrics through stakeholder interviews, website credibility repairs, centralized database infrastructure, and lead capture system implementation. Success means: Client has validated business metrics, a professional website, clean data foundation, and is capturing leads from website visitors.

---

### 2. Deliverables

Contractor will provide the following deliverables:

1. **ROI Assessment & Stakeholder Interviews** — 4 structured interviews (Sandy, Matthew, Alex, Scott) to gather baseline metrics, validate CODB, and document current processes

2. **Initial SOPs** — Documented workflows for Lead Handling and Lot Operations based on interview findings

3. **Website Quick Fixes** — Repair broken images, fix mobile layout, improve load times, add local SEO schema markup

4. **Database Foundation** — CEO-owned Firebase/cloud setup with Master Shed Data and CODB Calculator sheets, following strict data standards (snake_case, pure numbers)

5. **Customer Onboarding Form** — Mobile-friendly lead capture form with auto-responder and sales team notifications, deployed on homepage, contact page, and product pages

6. **Validation Workshop** — Co-creation session with Client to review findings and confirm priorities

---

### 3. Timeline

| Phase | Activities & Milestones |
|-------|------------------------|
| **Week 1** | Stakeholder interviews (Sandy 30min, Matthew 20min, Alex 15min, Scott 15min). Populate ROI Calculator. Draft initial SOPs. Website image audit and repairs. Mobile/SEO fixes. Validation workshop with Sandy. |
| **Week 2** | Firebase account setup (CEO-owned). Data migration to Master Shed Data sheet. CODB Calculator population. Onboarding form design, implementation, and deployment. Testing and handoff. |
| **Day 14+** | 14-day support period for questions and adjustments. |

---

### 4. Client Responsibilities

To ensure project success, Client agrees to:

1. Make key stakeholders available for interviews (approximately 90 minutes total across 4 people)
2. Participate in validation workshop (approximately 45 minutes)
3. Provide website admin/hosting credentials within 48 hours of project start
4. Provide existing inventory spreadsheets and financial/cost data
5. Create and own Firebase/Google Cloud account (Contractor will configure)
6. Respond to questions and review deliverables within 48 business hours
7. Provide email addresses for sales team notifications

### 5. Third-Party Tools & Services

- **Website Hosting** — Client's existing hosting service. Admin access required.
- **Form Service** — Contractor will recommend appropriate solution based on Client's tech stack.
