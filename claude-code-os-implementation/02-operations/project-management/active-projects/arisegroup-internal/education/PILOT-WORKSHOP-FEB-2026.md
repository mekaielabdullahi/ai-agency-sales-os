### Framework: The 10 Business Functions Model (15 min) - Mekaiel

| Time | Content |
|------|---------|
| 15:00-20:00 | Introducing the framework |
| 20:00-25:00 | Deep dive: 3 most AI-impacted functions |
| 25:00-30:00 | How to use this model for your business |

**Content**:
1. Present the 10 Business Functions wheel

### Interactive Exercise: Self-Audit (20 min) - Mekaiel + Katrina

| Time | Content |
|------|---------|
| 30:00-35:00 | Exercise introduction |
| 35:00-50:00 | Guided self-audit (workbook) |

**Exercise Structure**:
1. Provide downloadable worksheet
2. Guide attendees through mapping their OWN business functions
3. Have them identify:
   - Top 3 time-consuming functions
   - Where manual work happens daily
   - What frustrates them most
4. Katrina: Share entrepreneurship perspective during exercise
5. Mekaiel: Walk through one example live

**Chat Engagement Prompt**:
> "Drop in the chat: Which business function eats most of your time?"

### The "Aha Moment": AI Opportunity Matrix (15 min) - Mekaiel

| Time | Content |
|------|---------|
| 50:00-55:00 | Introduce the opportunity matrix |
| 55:00-65:00 | Map the quadrants |

**Key Moment** (minute 55):
> "You don't need AI everywhere. You need it in the 2-3 places where you're leaking time and money every day. Let me show you how to find those places in YOUR business."

**The Matrix**:
```
                    HIGH IMPACT
                        |
    STRATEGIC          |          QUICK WINS
    INVESTMENTS        |          (Start Here)
                       |
 ----------------------+----------------------
                       |
    NOT WORTH IT       |          NICE TO HAVE
                       |
                       |
                    LOW IMPACT

    HARD TO IMPLEMENT <---> EASY TO IMPLEMENT
```

**Content**:
1. Explain each quadrant
2. Show real examples from client work (anonymized)
3. Help attendees mentally place their pain points on the matrix
