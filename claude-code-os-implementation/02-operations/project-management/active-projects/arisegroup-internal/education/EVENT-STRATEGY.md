# Education Pillar: Event Lead Funnel Strategy

**Version**: 1.0
**Last Updated**: February 12, 2026
**Status**: Approved - Ready for Implementation

---

## Executive Summary

Events become our top-of-funnel feeding into the existing sales process: **Free Audit -> Paid Audit -> Implementation -> Retainer**. This strategy positions AriseGroup.ai as the authority on systematic AI adoption for small business founders.

**Core Value Proposition**: "Business owners are too busy doing the work to optimize. A 90-minute session unlocks scale potential and converts to paid engagements."

## Event Tier Architecture

| Tier | Format | Duration | Price | Purpose | Frequency |
|------|--------|----------|-------|---------|-----------|
| **Tier 0** | LinkedIn Live | 30-45 min | Free | Brand awareness, list building | Weekly |
| **Tier 1** | Virtual Networking | 75 min | Free | Community + qualification | Bi-weekly |
| **Tier 2** | Virtual Intensive | 3 hours | $47-97 | Deep education + conversion | Monthly |
| **Tier 3** | In-Person (w/ Katrina) | 3-4 hours | $197-297 | High-touch + conversion | Quarterly |


## Revenue Model

### Event Revenue (Direct)
- **Tier 1 (Free)**: $0 (lead gen investment)
- **Tier 2 ($47-97)**: $2K-3K/event (20-30 registrations)
- **Tier 3 ($197-297)**: $2K-4.5K/event (10-15 registrations)

### Conversion Revenue (Indirect)
- **Tier 1**: 5-8 audits -> 2 closes = $10K/event
- **Tier 2**: 3-5 audits -> 2 closes = $12K/event
- **Tier 3**: 4-6 audits -> 3 closes = $26K/event

### Annual Projection (After System Matures)
- **Tier 1 Monthly (x12)**: $120K pipeline
- **Tier 2 Monthly (x12)**: $144K pipeline
- **Tier 3 Quarterly (x4)**: $104K pipeline
- **Total Annual Potential**: ~$368K pipeline from events

## Technology Stack

### Required Integrations
1. **Eventbrite** -> Event hosting, ticketing, registration
2. **Eventbrite Ads** -> Paid promotion ($25/day, $170-188 total)
3. **Zapier** -> Automation hub
4. **Notion** -> Lead database (CRM)
5. **Gmail** -> Email sequences (or Mailchimp/ConvertKit)
6. **Calendly** -> Audit booking
7. **Zoom** -> Virtual event hosting

### Automation Workflow
```
Eventbrite Registration
      |
      v (Zapier)
Create Notion Lead Record
      |
      v (Zapier)
Add to Email Sequence (Pre-Event)
      |
      v (Zapier)
Tag: "Event: [Event Name]", "Status: Registered"

---

Post-Event:

Eventbrite Attendee Data
      |
      v (Zapier)
Update Notion: "Status: Attended" or "No-Show"
      |
      v (Zapier)
Trigger Post-Event Sequence
      |
      v (If Booked Audit)
Create Audit Task in Notion
```
