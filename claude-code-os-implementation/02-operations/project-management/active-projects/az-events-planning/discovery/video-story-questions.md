# Video Story Discovery Questions

## Purpose
Understand the story they want to tell so you can brief the video editor properly.

---

## The 10 Questions

### 1. Target Audience
> "When someone watches this video, who do you want them to be? A mom planning a birthday party? A bride planning a bachelorette? A corporate event planner?"

### 2. Core Message
> "If viewers remember just ONE thing after watching, what should that be?"

### 3. Emotional Tone
> "Should this video feel exciting and high-energy, or warm and trustworthy, or premium and exclusive?"

### 4. Bull Riding Positioning
> "For the bull riding content specifically - do you want to emphasize the thrill and excitement, or the safety and professionalism, or both equally?"

### 5. Safety Demonstration
> "I captured a lot of footage showing your safety practices. How prominently do you want safety featured? Background assurance or front-and-center selling point?"

### 6. Community/Foster Kids Story
> "I got some beautiful footage of the foster kids event. Do you want this as a separate video showcasing community involvement, or woven into the main brand video?"

### 7. Bachelorette Market Vibe
> "For the Scottsdale bachelorette market - what's the vibe? Classy and upscale? Fun and wild? Instagram-worthy?"

### 8. Call to Action
> "After watching, what do you want viewers to DO? Call immediately? Book online? Request a quote? Follow on social?"

### 9. Video Length & Platform
> "Where will this video live? Website hero? Instagram/TikTok? Facebook ads? YouTube? Different lengths for different platforms?"

### 10. Competitive Differentiation
> "What makes AZ Inflatable Events different from every other party rental company? What should make viewers choose YOU?"
