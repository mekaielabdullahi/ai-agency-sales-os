## üîç THE TWO-VERSION DOC QUESTION METHOD

### **VERSION 1: THE DIRECT PROBE (For Financially Savvy Clients)**

**Question Format:**  
*"On average, what does it cost you to operate your business for one day‚Äîincluding staff, equipment, marketing, everything?"*

**Follow-Up Probes:**
- "What's your biggest daily expense?"
- "How much of that daily cost is going toward growth vs. just keeping the lights on?"
- "If you could cut 10% off your daily operating cost without sacrificing quality, what would that be worth over a year?"

**What You're Testing:**
- ‚úÖ Do they know their numbers? (Good = specific answer)
- ‚ùå Are they guessing? (Bad = vague "uh, maybe $1,000?")
- ‚úÖ Do they track expenses by category?
- ‚ùå Do they lump everything together?

**If They Know (Good Sign):**
‚Üí They're data-driven, financially aware, ready for ROI conversations  
‚Üí Use specific numbers to show savings: *"So $800/day = $292K/year. If we save you 15% through automation, that's $43K back in your pocket."*

**If They Don't Know (Opportunity):**
‚Üí They're bleeding capital without realizing it  
‚Üí Position yourself as the diagnostic tool: *"Most business owners don't track this daily, which is why they can't see where they're losing money. Let's figure it out together."*

---

### **VERSION 2: THE BREAKDOWN METHOD (For Less Financial Clarity)**

**Question Format:**  
*"Let's break this down‚Äîhow much are you spending daily on [specific category]?"*

**Step-by-Step Breakdown Questions:**

1. **"How many people are on your team, and what's your total monthly payroll?"**
   - Monthly payroll √∑ 30 = **Daily Labor Cost**
   - *"So roughly $XXX per day just in wages. Got it."*

2. **"How much are you spending on marketing per month‚Äîads, website, social media, etc.?"**
   - Monthly marketing √∑ 30 = **Daily Marketing Cost**
   - *"Okay, so about $XXX/day to bring in customers."*

3. **"What about equipment‚Äîfuel, maintenance, repairs, replacements? What does that run you monthly?"**
   - Monthly equipment costs √∑ 30 = **Daily Equipment Cost**
   - *"So around $XXX/day keeping everything running."*

4. **"How much time does your team spend each day on scheduling, customer follow-up, and admin work?"**
   - Hours √ó hourly rate = **Daily Admin Cost**
   - *"That's $XXX/day just on tasks that could be automated."*

5. **"How many inquiries do you get per day, and how many don't convert because of slow response or availability issues?"**
   - Lost bookings √ó avg booking value √∑ 30 = **Daily Opportunity Cost**
   - *"So you're potentially losing $XXX/day from missed opportunities."*

**TOTAL DAILY OPERATING COST:**
= Labor + Marketing + Equipment + Admin + Opportunity Cost

**The Reveal:**
*"Okay, so just from these categories alone, you're spending roughly $XXX per day to run the business. That's $XXX,XXX per year. Now let's talk about where you're hemorrhaging money without realizing it..."*
