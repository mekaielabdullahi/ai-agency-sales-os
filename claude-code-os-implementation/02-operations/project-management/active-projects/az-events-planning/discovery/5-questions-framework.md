# 5 Questions Framework - AZ Inflatable Events

## The Method
Ask in order. Listen more than talk. Their answers become your proposal.

---

## Q1: What is the biggest problem in your business right now?

**Customized for AZ Inflatable:**
- "What's the biggest operational challenge you're facing as you look to expand into bull riding?"
- "What's taking up the most time in your day-to-day operations right now?"
- "As you're growing the business, what's the bottleneck that's slowing you down?"

**Listen for:**
- Scheduling chaos
- Inventory management
- Communication gaps
- Manual processes
- Customer service overload
- Equipment tracking
- Crew coordination

---

## Q2: How much is this problem costing the business per month?

**Customized for AZ Inflatable:**
- "If you had to estimate, how much revenue are you losing or leaving on the table each month because of [their Q1 answer]?"
- "How many booking inquiries don't convert because of delays in response time?"
- "How much time does your team spend on tasks that could be automated?"
- "What's the cost of double-bookings, scheduling errors, or missed opportunities?"

**Listen for:**
- Dollar amounts (even rough)
- Time waste (convert to $)
- Lost bookings
- Inefficiencies
- Crew overtime

---

## Q3: How long have you had this problem?

**Customized for AZ Inflatable:**
- "How long have you been dealing with this issue?"
- "Have you tried any solutions to fix this?"
- "What's stopped you from solving this before now?"
- "Is this getting better, worse, or staying the same?"

**Listen for:**
- Chronic vs new problem
- Previous attempts
- Why haven't fixed yet
- Amy's recovery impact

---

## Q4: If you do nothing, what does the next 6-12 months look like?

**Customized for AZ Inflatable:**
- "If nothing changes over the next 6-12 months, especially as you're adding bull riding to your offerings, what does that look like for the business?"
- "How does this impact your ability to scale?"
- "What happens to your team's workload?"
- "What does this mean for customer experience and retention?"

**Listen for:**
- Growth ceiling
- Team burnout
- Customer churn
- Competitive disadvantage
- Missed revenue
- Bull riding launch at risk

---

## Q5: If I had a magic wand, what would the perfect solution look like?

**Customized for AZ Inflatable:**
- "If you could wave a magic wand and have the perfect system in place—one that completely solved [their Q1 problem]—what would that look like day-to-day?"
- "What would your team be doing instead of [manual task]?"
- "How would customers interact with you differently?"
- "What would success look like 90 days from now?"

**Listen for:**
- Their ideal solution
- Success metrics
- What THEY value most
- Dealbreakers
- Must-haves

---

## Post-Discovery: What You Now Have

After Q1-Q5, you know:
- [ ] The problem (Q1)
- [ ] What they'll pay / value (Q2)
- [ ] How urgent it is (Q3)
- [ ] Why they must act now (Q4)
- [ ] Exactly what to build/propose (Q5)

**Their answers ARE your proposal.**
