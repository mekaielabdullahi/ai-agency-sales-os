## 5 QUESTIONS (In Order)

1. **What's your biggest problem right now?**
2. **How much is it costing you per month?**
3. **How long have you had this problem?**
4. **If nothing changes in 6-12 months, what happens?**
5. **Magic wand - what would the perfect solution look like?**

## BUYING SIGNALS (Green Lights)
- Specific answers to Q1 (real problem)
- Dollar amounts for Q2 (they value it)
- Frustrated tone on Q3 (chronic pain)
- Urgent tone on Q4 (fear of inaction)
- Excited/detailed on Q5 (they want change)

---

## RED FLAGS
- "No problems" → No pain = no sale
- Can't quantify cost → Don't value it enough
- "Tried everything" → Change-resistant
- Vague Q5 answers → Don't know what they want

---
