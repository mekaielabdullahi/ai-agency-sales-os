# Solution: Andrew Supplies Integration

## Problem Statement

Andrew recently joined Plotter Mechanix with proven supplies distribution expertise, but lacks the technology infrastructure to scale his knowledge across 10,000 contacts. These contacts are currently buying supplies elsewhere, representing significant missed revenue opportunity.

## Solution Overview

Build adaptive technology infrastructure tailored to Andrew's workflows, enabling him to leverage his 25 years of HP Partner experience across the entire customer base. Our role is to multiply his effectiveness through automation while he focuses on strategy and relationships.

## Implementation Details

### Discovery Process (Week 1)

**Initial Discovery Session (1.5-2 hours)**
After Andrew has been with Plotter Mechanix for 1-2 weeks:
- Current integration with Plotter Mechanix workflows
- Pain points and friction areas
- Technology gaps and needs
- Automation opportunities
- 30-60 day priorities

### Adaptive Solutions (Based on Discovery)

**Potential Technology Infrastructure:**
- Customer equipment database (track what each contact owns)
- Automated supplies recommendations based on equipment
- Reorder reminder system with predictive timing
- Margin optimization tools
- Direct integration with Andrew's supplier network
- Bundle service + supplies quotes
- Ply-Jobber sync optimization

**The Math That Matters:**
- 10,000 contacts buying supplies elsewhere
- Average customer: $500-2,000/year in supplies
