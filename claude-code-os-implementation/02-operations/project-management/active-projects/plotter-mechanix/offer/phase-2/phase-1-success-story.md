### The Root Cause
Every customer touchpoint required **manual steps** to reach Jobber:
- Customer calls Kelsey ‚Üí Kelsey texts Alyssa ‚Üí Alyssa enters in Jobber
- Customer emails ‚Üí Lost in 206-message inbox ‚Üí Maybe gets to Jobber
- Field notes ‚Üí Sticky notes ‚Üí Phone call ‚Üí Alyssa transcribes

**Result:** Lag time + errors = trust erosion
