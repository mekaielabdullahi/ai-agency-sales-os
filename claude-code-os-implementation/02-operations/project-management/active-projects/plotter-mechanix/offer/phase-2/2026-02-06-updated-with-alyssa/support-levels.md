## What Each Element Means

### Check-in Calls
Scheduled video calls to review progress, discuss priorities, and address blockers.
- Foundation: Status update focus
- Growth: Strategy + status
- Full Scale: Deep strategic alignment

### Response Time
How quickly you can expect a response to emails or Slack messages.
- Foundation: Within 2 business days
- Growth: Same business day (usually within a few hours)
- Full Scale: Within 4-6 hours during business hours

### Team Hours
The approximate hours our team dedicates to your project each week.
- This is an estimate, not a guarantee
- Actual hours may vary based on project phase
- Includes all work: development, meetings, documentation

### Queue Priority
When requests come in, this determines the order we address them.
- Foundation: Processed in order received, alongside other projects
- Growth: Moved ahead of Foundation tier requests
- Full Scale: First to be addressed, always

### Post-Launch Support
After we complete the main engagement:
- Bug fixes for issues in delivered work
- Minor adjustments and tweaks
- Training support for your team
- Does NOT include new feature development

## What's NOT Included

Regardless of tier, support does NOT include:
- New feature development beyond original scope
- Third-party system issues outside our control
- Training for staff turnover (beyond post-launch period)
- Hardware or infrastructure issues
- Changes to scope without change order
