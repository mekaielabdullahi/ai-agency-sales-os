Topics

Plotter Mechanics' Business Model & Challenges

- Core Business: A service-first model focused on printer repair and support for other dealers, supplemented by sales of used equipment and a private-label ink line.
- Key Bottleneck: Chris Andrade is the sole expert technician, creating a burnout risk and preventing focus on strategic growth.
- Growth Opportunities:
  - Lucid Motors Contract: A potential high-volume film cutting service (200 cuts/day) that cannot be pursued due to current operational constraints.
  - Consumables Automation: Scaling the private-label ink business via an automated, drop-shipped e-commerce model.
  - Rental Program: Expanding a successful, high-revenue rental service ($35k from one customer last year).
- Operational Gaps:
  - Fragmented Systems: Disconnected tools (Jobber, QuickBooks, Shopify, Dropbox) cause data silos and inefficiencies.
  - Poor Communication: Critical information from field calls and texts is not consistently captured, leading to missed tasks and poor coordination with the office.
  - Inventory Management: Disorganized inventory and a lack of clear processes for RMAs and internal use cause stockouts and financial inaccuracies.

Arise Group AI's Proposed Solution

- Team: Arise Group AI is an engineering-as-a-service agency formed by Trent Christopher (industrial automation), Matthew Kerns (software engineering), and Mekaiel Abdullahi (IT/process).
- Initial "Quick Win" Phase: A 30-day, fixed-price engagement to deliver immediate, tangible relief and build trust.
  - Primary Tool: Implement Quo, a business phone system that will replace the current Vonage service.
  - Quo's Functionality:
    - Centralized Comms: Port the main business number to Quo, consolidating all calls and texts.
    - Call Routing: Automatically route calls to the correct person (e.g., admin to Alyssa, printing to Joe).
    - Transcription & Automation: Record and transcribe all business calls and texts.
      - Goal: Use AI to automatically create tasks, quotes, and Jobber entries from spoken words.
- Long-Term Vision:
  - Knowledge Capture: Systematically record Andrade's expertise (audio, video) to create a searchable knowledge base and SOPs for training new technicians.
  - Data Integration: Build a "command center" that unifies data from all systems (Jobber, QuickBooks, email) into a single, actionable dashboard.
  - Automated Pricing: Use AI to read vendor price lists (even unstructured PDFs) and automatically update pricing across all systems.
  - Productization: Develop the internal system into a proprietary "Plotter Mechanics Hub" software that could eventually be sold to other businesses.
