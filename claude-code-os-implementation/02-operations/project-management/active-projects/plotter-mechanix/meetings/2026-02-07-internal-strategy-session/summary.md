## Strategic Decisions

### 1. Sales Strategy: "Foot-in-the-Door" Approach

**Problem:** Need to secure initial client revenue to fund operations and build trust for larger engagements.

**Solution:** Offer small, low-cost "Trojan horse" projects to demonstrate value quickly.

**Implementation:**
- Lead with $500-$1k knowledge base agents or similar quick wins
- Ask for client's budget upfront, then scope project to fit
- Use success of initial project to naturally expand into larger engagements

**Rationale:** Builds trust incrementally and creates a natural path to bigger projects as clients see results.
