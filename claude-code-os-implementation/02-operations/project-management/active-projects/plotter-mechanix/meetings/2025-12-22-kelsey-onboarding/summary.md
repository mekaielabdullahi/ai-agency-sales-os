### Implementing Quo for Unified Communication

**Trent's Explanation:**
> "Implementing Quo for centralized communication and call/text routing"

**Quo Purpose:**
- Replace/supplement Vonage
- Centralize all business calls and texts
- Auto-sync to Jobber
- Call screening and routing
- AI transcription of all calls

**How It Helps:**
- Port business number to Quo
- Calls automatically transcribed
- Summaries auto-logged to Jobber
- Reduces manual data entry
- Nothing falls through cracks

**Expected Outcomes:**
