# Remus Development - Discovery Framework

## Frame the Session

> "We're going to identify where money, time, and trust are leaking — and what must be stabilized first."

---

## Discovery Pillars

### 1. Revenue Flow
**Bids → Contracts → Cash**

Questions:
- [ ] How many bids in last 90 days?
- [ ] How many converted to contracts?
- [ ] What's your close rate?
- [ ] Where do deals stall?
- [ ] Average time from bid to signed contract?
- [ ] What killed the Fiesta job specifically?

---

### 2. Decision Bottlenecks
**What waits on Mike?**

Questions:
- [ ] List 5 decisions that required you this week
- [ ] Which could someone else have made?
- [ ] What's the cost when you're unavailable?
- [ ] Who can approve what without you?
- [ ] What's your threshold for "needs my eyes"?

---

### 3. Execution Gaps
**Where things fall through**

Questions:
- [ ] Last 3 things that slipped through the cracks?
- [ ] Who was supposed to own each?
- [ ] What would have caught it earlier?
- [ ] Where do handoffs break down?
- [ ] What's the cost of each dropped ball?

---

### 4. Risk Exposure
**Contracts & compliance**

Questions:
- [ ] How many contracts reviewed in last 30 days?
- [ ] Who reviews them?
- [ ] What clauses worry you most?
- [ ] Have you lost money due to contract issues?
- [ ] What would a red-flag checklist look like?

---

### 5. Visibility
**What Mike can't see today**

Questions:
- [ ] What do you wish you could see on a single screen?
- [ ] What surprises you (bad news coming late)?
- [ ] What data exists but isn't accessible?
- [ ] What do you have to ask someone to find out?

---

### 6. Automation Opportunities
**Only after the above**

Questions:
- [ ] What's most repetitive and painful?
- [ ] What would you pay to never do again?
- [ ] What's high-value if done consistently?
- [ ] What's low-value but still required?
