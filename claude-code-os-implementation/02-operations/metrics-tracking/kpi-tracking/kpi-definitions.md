# AriseGroup.ai KPI Definitions & Targets

**Effective:** December 13, 2025
**Review Cycle:** Weekly (Fridays)

---

## Core KPIs

### 1. REVENUE KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Daily Target | Weekly Target | Why It Matters |
|-----|------------|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Cash Closed** | Money received in bank | - | Track actuals | Survival metric |
| **Deals Closed** | Signed contracts | - | 1+ | Pipeline health |
| **Pipeline Value** | Total $ of active prospects | - | Growing | Future revenue |
| **Avg Deal Size** | Revenue / Deals | - | Track trend | Efficiency metric |

**Pipeline Stages:**
1. **Lead** - Identified, not contacted
2. **Contacted** - Outreach sent
3. **Engaged** - Responded, conversation started
4. **Discovery** - Call scheduled or completed
5. **Proposal** - Sent proposal/quote
6. **Negotiation** - Discussing terms
7. **Closed Won** - Signed
8. **Closed Lost** - Dead

---

### 5. TIME & FOCUS KPIs

| KPI | Definition | Daily Target | Weekly Target | Why It Matters |
|-----|------------|--------------|---------------|----------------|
| **Revenue Hours** | Time on sales + delivery | **4 hrs** | **20 hrs** | Productivity |
| **Admin Hours** | Non-revenue work | <2 hrs | <10 hrs | Efficiency |
| **Focus Score** | Self-rated 1-10 | **8+** | **8+ avg** | Quality of work |
| **Deep Work Blocks** | 90+ min focused sessions | **2** | **10** | Output quality |

**Revenue Hours Include:**
- Outreach and sales conversations
- Discovery calls
- Proposal creation
- Client delivery work
- Content creation (builds pipeline)

**Admin Hours Include:**
- Email management
- Tool setup
- Internal operations
- Meetings (non-sales)

## Tracking Cadence

### Daily (5 min, end of day)
- Log outreach count
- Log calls/meetings
- Log revenue hours
- Rate focus score
- Note any deals moved

### Weekly (15 min, Friday)
- Sum all daily metrics
- Calculate response rate
- Update pipeline value
- Review targets vs actuals
- Set next week focus

### Monthly (30 min, last Friday)
- Revenue closed vs target
- Pipeline growth trend
- Content performance
- Delivery quality
- Adjust targets if needed

## Red Flags (Intervene Immediately)

| Situation | Action |
|-----------|--------|
| 0 outreach for 3+ days | Stop everything, send 10 messages |
| 0 discovery calls in a week | Audit outreach quality, increase volume |
| Pipeline value decreasing | Emergency outreach sprint |
| Focus score <6 for 3+ days | Environmental/energy audit |
| Revenue hours <3/day | Block calendar, eliminate distractions |

---

## Formula Reference

```
Response Rate = Responses / Outreach Sent × 100
Close Rate = Deals Closed / Discovery Calls × 100
Pipeline Velocity = Pipeline Value / Avg Sales Cycle Days
Revenue per Hour = Cash Closed / Revenue Hours
```
