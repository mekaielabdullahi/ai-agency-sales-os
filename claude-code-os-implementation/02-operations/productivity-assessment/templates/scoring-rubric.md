## ðŸš¨ Warning Signs & Interventions

### Consistent Low Scores (5-6/10 for 5+ days)
**Causes**:
- Wrong priorities
- Unrealistic planning
- External blockers
- Energy management issues

**Intervention**:
- Strategic realignment check
- Capacity reality check
- Blocker identification
- Energy audit

### Score Volatility (swinging 3+ points daily)
**Causes**:
- Inconsistent routines
- Poor planning
- Unmanaged interruptions
- Energy mismanagement

**Intervention**:
- Standardize morning routine
- Improve daily planning
- Set boundaries
- Match energy to tasks

### Specific Component Consistently Low
**Task Completion Low**: Overplanning, wrong priorities
**Strategic Alignment Low**: OBG unclear, strategic drift
**Time Efficiency Low**: Poor estimation, scope creep
**Focus Quality Low**: Environment issues, boundaries
