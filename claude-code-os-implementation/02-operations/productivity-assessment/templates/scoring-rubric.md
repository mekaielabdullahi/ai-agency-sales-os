# Productivity Scoring Rubric

## Daily Score (1-10)

### Score Components
| Component | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Task Completion | 40% | Tier 1 tasks completed vs planned |
| Strategic Alignment | 30% | Work served OBG ($50k/mo revenue) |
| Time Efficiency | 20% | Actual vs estimated time |
| Focus Quality | 10% | Deep work blocks maintained |

---

## Score Definitions

### 10/10 - Exceptional
- All Tier 1 tasks completed
- Made significant progress on Tier 2
- 100% strategically aligned (P0 revenue work)
- Time estimates within 10%
- All focus blocks maintained
- THE ONE THING completed with excellence

### 9/10 - Excellent
- All Tier 1 tasks completed
- Some Tier 2 progress
- Fully aligned with OBG
- Time estimates within 15%
- Most focus blocks maintained

### 8/10 - Very Good
- All Tier 1 tasks completed
- Work aligned with OBG
- Time estimates within 20%
- Focus generally maintained
- Minor drift, quickly corrected

### 7/10 - Good
- Most Tier 1 tasks completed (80%+)
- Mostly aligned with OBG
- Some time estimation variance
- Acceptable focus quality
- Some non-essential work crept in

### 6/10 - Acceptable
- Majority of Tier 1 completed (60-80%)
- Generally aligned, some drift
- Time estimates off by 30%+
- Focus interrupted multiple times
- Spent time on lower priorities

### 5/10 - Below Average
- Half of Tier 1 completed
- Significant strategic drift
- Poor time estimation
- Focus frequently broken
- Worked on wrong things

### 4/10 - Poor
- Less than half Tier 1 completed
- Mostly misaligned work
- Major time miscalculation
- Very little deep focus
- Avoided important work

### 3/10 - Very Poor
- Minimal Tier 1 completion
- Worked on anti-priorities
- Day felt wasted
- No real progress on OBG

### 2/10 - Failed
- Almost nothing completed
- Completely off-track
- Major blockers not addressed
- Procrastination dominated

### 1/10 - Total Loss
- Nothing productive accomplished
- Complete strategic misalignment
- Day lost to distractions/avoidance

---

## Quick Scoring Guide

### For Task Completion (40%)
```
100% Tier 1 done = 10 points
90% Tier 1 done = 9 points
80% Tier 1 done = 8 points
... and so on
```

### For Strategic Alignment (30%)
```
All work served $50k/mo goal (P0-P1) = 10 points
Most work served goal = 8 points
Half served goal = 5 points
Mostly off-track (P3+ work) = 3 points
Worked on anti-priorities = 1 point
```

### For Time Efficiency (20%)
```
Within 10% of estimates = 10 points
Within 20% = 8 points
Within 30% = 6 points
Within 50% = 4 points
Way off = 2 points
```

### For Focus Quality (10%)
```
All focus blocks maintained = 10 points
Most maintained = 8 points
Half maintained = 5 points
Constantly interrupted = 3 points
No deep focus achieved = 1 point
```

## Weekly Targets

| Score Range | Interpretation | Action |
|-------------|----------------|--------|
| 8-10 avg | On track | Maintain momentum |
| 7-8 avg | Good, room to improve | Minor adjustments |
| 6-7 avg | Warning zone | Review priorities |
| <6 avg | Intervention needed | Strategic reset |

---

## Honest Scoring Rules

1. **No inflation** - Be brutally honest
2. **Evidence-based** - Score based on facts, not feelings
3. **OBG-centered** - $50k/mo revenue is the goal
4. **Learn from lows** - Low scores are data, not failure
5. **Trend matters** - One bad day is fine, patterns aren't
