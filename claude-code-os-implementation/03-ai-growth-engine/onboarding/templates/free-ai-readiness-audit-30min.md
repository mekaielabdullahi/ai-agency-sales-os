# Free AI Readiness Audit Framework (30-Minute Discovery Call)

**Purpose**: Qualify prospects, identify AI opportunities, demonstrate value, convert to paying clients

**Use Case**: Pre-sale prospecting / Lead magnet / Discovery calls
**Duration**: 30 minutes
**Deliverable**: Framework conversation + optional 1-2 page summary (post-call)

- "What's a typical day/week look like for you?"
- "Where does most of your time go?"
- "What are your biggest operational challenges right now?"
- "If you could fix one thing about how your business runs, what would it be?"

- "Walk me through [specific process] step-by-step."
- "Who's involved in [process]?"
- "What tools/systems do you use for [process]?"
- "Where does [process] break down or slow down?"

- "Does this resonate with what you're experiencing?"

## QUALIFICATION CRITERIA (Is This a Good Fit?)

### Good Fit Signals ‚úÖ
- Clear pain points they can articulate
- Willing to invest in solutions (budget exists)
- Open to change (not resistant to new tools)
- Decision-maker or close to decision-maker
- Timeline for implementation (next 3-6 months)
- ICP match (20-100 employees, small business/nonprofit)
- Growth-minded (scaling, not just maintaining)

### Red Flags ‚ùå
- Vague pain ("things could be better" vs specific issues)
- No budget or "looking for free solutions"
- Resistant to change ("we've always done it this way")
- Not decision-maker and no path to DM
- No urgency (timeline is "someday")
- Outside ICP (too small, too large, wrong industry)
- Just kicking tires / collecting info

### Borderline (Nurture) üü°
- Interest but not urgency (revisit in 3-6 months)
- Budget exists but allocated elsewhere (follow up next quarter)
- Fit but timing is off (stay connected)
- Need internal buy-in first (offer to help with that)

## SUCCESS METRICS

### Per Call Metrics
- Call completion rate (showed up vs no-show): Target 80%+
- Conversion to next step: Target 30-40%
  - Hot (proposal/engagement): 10-20%
  - Warm (follow-up scheduled): 20-30%
  - Cold (not a fit): 40-50%
- Referrals received: Target 10-20%
- Time per call (total): Target <90 min (15 prep + 30 call + 30 follow-up)

### Weekly Metrics (Once Scaled)
- Discovery calls completed: Target 3-5/week
- Proposals sent: Target 1-2/week
- Deals closed: Target 1-2/month

### Quality Metrics
- Prospect satisfaction: Qualitative (did they find it valuable?)
- Follow-through rate: % who complete next step
- Response to follow-up: % who respond to summary email

## CONTINUOUS IMPROVEMENT

### After Each Call, Ask Yourself:
- What questions uncovered the most valuable insights?
- What pain points are I hearing repeatedly?
- What opportunities resonate most with prospects?
- Where did I lose them or confuse them?
- What would I do differently next time?

### After 5 Calls, Review:
- What patterns am I seeing across prospects?
- What questions should I add/remove?
- What messaging is working vs not working?
- Should I adjust my ICP?
- What objections am I hearing?

### After 10 Calls, Optimize:
- Update this framework based on learnings
- Create templates for common pain points
- Refine AI opportunity positioning
- Adjust pricing/packaging based on feedback
- Consider: What should be automated in this process?
