## Update Cadence

### Scheduled Updates

You'll receive progress updates on:

| Day | Time | What You'll Get |
|-----|------|-----------------|
| **Tuesday** | End of day | Progress update: what we accomplished, what's next |
| **Friday** | End of day | Weekly summary: week's progress, next week's plan |

**Format:** Slack message in your channel with:
- What we completed this period
- What's planned next
- Any blockers or questions for you
- Overall project health (on track / needs attention)

### You Don't Need to Respond

These updates are FYI. No response needed unless:
- You have questions
- Something doesn't look right
- You want to add/change something

---

## What to Expect From Me

1. **Proactive communication** - You won't need to chase me for updates
2. **Honest timelines** - If something's delayed, you'll know immediately
3. **Clear next steps** - Every update includes what happens next
4. **Quick responses** - During availability windows, I'm responsive
5. **No surprises** - Blockers get flagged early, not at deadline

---

## What I Need From You

1. **Timely responses** - Especially for access requests and approvals
2. **Heads up on unavailability** - So we can plan around it
3. **Questions over assumptions** - If something's unclear, ask
4. **Honest feedback** - If something's not right, tell me early
