# Lead Qualification Template

## Purpose
Filter scored leads to ensure the sales team only engages with real buyers experiencing workflow pain points that automation can solve.

---

## Qualification Prerequisites

Before beginning qualification:
- [ ] Lead has ICP Score of 14 or greater (P1 or P2)
- [ ] Lead record is complete in CRM
- [ ] Basic research has been completed

**If prerequisites not met:** Do not proceed with qualification

---

## The 5 Qualification Criteria

A lead must meet ALL FIVE criteria to be considered qualified.

- [ ] Customer success stories on website

### Qualification Outcome

- [ ] **QUALIFIED** (5/5 criteria met) → Approve for Discovery Call
- [ ] **BORDERLINE** (4/5 criteria met) → Sales Lead Review Required
- [ ] **DISQUALIFIED** (3 or fewer criteria met) → Archive Lead

---

## Next Steps Based on Outcome

### If QUALIFIED (5/5):
1. [ ] Update CRM status to "Qualified"
2. [ ] Tag lead as "Discovery Ready"
3. [ ] Assign to sales rep for outreach
4. [ ] Begin discovery call booking sequence
5. [ ] Add to "Active Pipeline" view
6. [ ] Set follow-up reminder (24-48 hours)

### If BORDERLINE (4/5):
1. [ ] Document which criterion was not met
2. [ ] Determine if additional research could confirm missing criterion
3. [ ] Schedule sales team review (15-min discussion)
4. [ ] Make final decision within 24 hours
5. [ ] Either proceed to Qualified or move to Disqualified

### If DISQUALIFIED (≤3/5):
1. [ ] Update CRM status to "Disqualified"
2. [ ] Document disqualification reason
3. [ ] Add to "Archive - Revisit Quarterly" list
4. [ ] Do NOT book discovery call
5. [ ] Remove from active sales queue
6. [ ] Set reminder to revisit in 90 days

## Quality Control Checklist

Before finalizing qualification:
- [ ] All 5 criteria evaluated thoroughly
- [ ] Evidence documented for each criterion
- [ ] Disqualification reasons documented (if applicable)
- [ ] CRM updated with qualification status
- [ ] Next steps assigned and scheduled
- [ ] Sales team notified of decision
- [ ] Qualification completed within 24 hours of ICP scoring

---

## Common Qualification Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ **Aspirational Qualification:** Don't qualify based on potential future state
2. ❌ **Skipping Criteria:** All 5 must be evaluated, not just 3-4
3. ❌ **Pressure to Qualify:** Don't force a lead through if criteria not met
4. ❌ **Ignoring Red Flags:** If something feels off, investigate further
5. ❌ **Incomplete Documentation:** Always document reasoning
6. ❌ **Proceeding with Borderline:** Get approval before moving forward

---

## Qualification Speed Guidelines

**Target Timeframes:**
- P1 Lead Qualification: Within 4 hours of ICP scoring
- P2 Lead Qualification: Within 24 hours of ICP scoring
- Borderline Review: Within 24 hours of flagging
- Total ICP Score → Qualification → Outreach: Within 48 hours

---

## Red Flag Override

**STOP qualification immediately if:**
- Lead is competitor researching our process
- Lead has history of non-payment or disputes (research required)
- Lead requests services outside our scope
- Lead shows signs of being uncoachable or difficult
- Legal/ethical concerns about the business

**Action:** Flag for senior sales leadership review before proceeding
