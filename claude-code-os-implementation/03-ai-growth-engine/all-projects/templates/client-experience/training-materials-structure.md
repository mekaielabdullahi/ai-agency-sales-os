# Training Materials Structure & Templates

## Purpose
Provide clients with comprehensive, accessible training that ensures successful adoption and long-term value from their automation systems.

**Delivery Timeline:** 1-3 days after production deployment

---

## Training Delivery Philosophy

### Our Training Approach

**1. Show, Don't Just Tell**
- Video walkthroughs > written docs (when possible)
- Real examples > hypothetical scenarios
- Visual diagrams > text descriptions

**2. Just-In-Time Learning**
- Focus on what they'll use immediately
- Advanced features come later
- Reference materials for "when you need it"

**3. Multiple Learning Formats**
- Live training sessions (interactive)
- Video library (rewatchable)
- Written documentation (searchable)
- Quick reference guides (printable)

---

## 1. Live Training Session Structure

### Session 1: System Overview & Core Features (90 min)

**Pre-Session:**
- [ ] Client team invited (all users)
- [ ] Training environment prepared
- [ ] Sample data loaded
- [ ] Screen share tested
- [ ] Recording enabled

---

#### Segment 1: Welcome & Context (10 min)

**Script:**
> "Welcome everyone! Over the next 90 minutes, we're going to walk through your new automation system. By the end, you'll know how to use it, monitor it, and troubleshoot basic issues.
>
> We're recording this session, so you can rewatch it anytime. Feel free to interrupt with questionsâ€”this is interactive!"

**Cover:**
- What was built and why
- High-level system architecture (simple diagram)
- Expected outcomes and benefits

---

#### Segment 2: System Walkthrough (40 min)

**For Each Automation:**

**1. Show the Automation in Action (5 min)**
- Trigger a real example
- Walk through what's happening behind the scenes
- Show the end result

**Example:**
> "Let's capture a lead from your website form right now. I'll submit a form... [submits]
>
> Watch what happens: The lead instantly appears in your CRM, you got an SMS notification, and the lead received an automated email. All of that happened in under 5 seconds, with zero manual work."

**2. Show the Client-Facing View (10 min)**
- Where they see data (CRM, reports, etc.)
- How to interpret what they're seeing
- What "normal" looks like

**3. Common Tasks (5 min)**
- How to [perform common action]
- How to [check status]
- How to [manually override if needed]

---

#### Segment 3: Monitoring & Health Checks (15 min)

**Cover:**
- How to check if the system is running (dashboard, logs, etc.)
- What "healthy" looks like
- Red flags to watch for
- When to contact support

**Hands-On:**
Client performs health check themselves while you guide.

---

#### Segment 4: Troubleshooting Basics (15 min)

**Cover Top 3 Issues:**

**Issue 1: [Common Issue]**
- How to identify it
- How to fix it (step-by-step)
- When to escalate to support

**Issue 2: [Common Issue]**
- How to identify it
- How to fix it
- When to escalate

**Issue 3: [Common Issue]**
- How to identify it
- How to fix it
- When to escalate

---

#### Segment 5: Q&A & Next Steps (10 min)

- Open floor for questions
- Recap key takeaways
- Share link to video library and documentation
- Explain ongoing support options

---

### Session 2: Advanced Features & Optimization (Optional - 30 min)

For clients who want to go deeper:

- Advanced reporting and analytics
- Custom adjustments they can make
- Integration with other tools
- Scaling considerations
