### DAILY OUTREACH ROUTINE

Execute this every business day before anything else:

```
MORNING BLOCK (60-90 min):
1. Check CRM for follow-ups due today (15 min)
2. Send all scheduled follow-ups FIRST (15 min)
3. Research 3-5 new prospects (15 min)
4. Send 3-5 new outreach messages (30 min)
5. Log everything in CRM immediately

DO NOT skip follow-ups to send new outreach.
```

**Weekly targets per founder:**
- New outreach messages: 15-20
- Follow-ups sent: 10-15
- Calls booked: 3-5

# NURTURE SEQUENCE

**For leads that went cold at any stage. The goal: stay top of mind until they're ready.**

Every lead that doesn't close goes into nurture. No lead gets forgotten. This is where future revenue lives.

---

## Nurture Segments

Tag every lead entering nurture with their last stage:

| Tag | Meaning | Temperature |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| `outreach-cold` | Never responded to outreach | Cold |
| `discovery-no-show` | Booked but didn't show | Warm |
| `discovery-complete` | Had call, declined audit | Warm |
| `audit-declined` | Received audit offer, didn't buy | Warm |
| `proposal-declined` | Received proposal, didn't close | Hot |
| `timing-not-now` | Explicitly said "not now" | Warm |

# KEY RULES

1. **Follow-ups are non-negotiable.** Most deals close on follow-up #2-4, not the first message. Never skip them.
2. **Speed wins.** Respond within 2 hours. Send invoices within 1 hour. Send proposals within 24 hours. Slow = lost deal.
3. **Every "no" goes to nurture.** No lead is dead. They're just "not yet." The nurture sequence keeps them warm.
4. **Log everything.** If it's not in the CRM, it didn't happen. Update after every touchpoint.
5. **Don't pitch in follow-ups.** Follow-ups remove friction. They ask questions. They don't re-sell.
6. **The audit is the entry point.** Every discovery call should lead to an audit pitch. The audit creates the proposal. The proposal creates the deal.
7. **Credit the audit fee.** Always credit the audit investment toward implementation. It removes a buying objection and increases conversion.
