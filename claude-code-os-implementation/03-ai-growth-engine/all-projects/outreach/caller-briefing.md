## Your Job on the Call

**You are NOT selling. You are qualifying.**

Your goal on every call is to answer one question:
> "Does this business have a real, painful, expensive problem that AI could solve?"

If yes → book a 30-minute discovery call with Mekaiel.
If no → politely end and move on.

---

## The Script — 5 Questions Method

### Opening (first 20 seconds)

```
"Hey [Name], this is [Your Name] calling on behalf of Mekaiel at Arise Group.

We work with [their industry] businesses to help them automate the stuff that's eating their time.

I'm not going to pitch you anything — I just have one quick question.

Do you have 60 seconds?"
```

**If they say no:** "No problem — when's a better time to call back?" (Schedule a callback, don't push)

**If they say yes:** Move to Q1.

---

### Q1 — The Only Question That Matters

```
"What's the biggest problem in your business right now?"
```

**Then SHUT UP. Let them talk.**

- If they say "we're fine" → "Totally get it. Most businesses I talk to say the same thing before they realize how much time they're losing to admin. What does your team spend the most time on each week?"
- If they're vague → "Can you give me an example of what that looks like day-to-day?"
- If they open up → go to Q2

---

### Q2 — Quantify the Pain

```
"Got it. Rough ballpark — how much do you think that's costing you per month?
Could be lost revenue, staff time, missed deals — whatever way makes sense."
```

**Push gently if they're vague:**
```
"Is it more like $1-5K a month or more in the $10K+ range?"
```

- If <$3K/month → probably not a fit, wrap up politely
- If $5K+/month → qualified, keep going

---

### Q3 — Urgency Check

```
"And how long has this been going on?"
```

- If recent → "So it's getting worse?"
- If chronic → "So you've been dealing with this for [X months/years]?"

---

### Q4 — Consequence (This is where urgency lives)

```
"If nothing changes — what does the next 6-12 months look like for you?"
```

**Let them feel it. Don't interrupt.**

---

### Q5 — The Handoff Setup

```
"Last question — if you could wave a magic wand and fix this problem perfectly, what would that look like?"
```

After they answer:

```
"That's exactly the kind of thing we build. Mekaiel works with a handful of businesses at a time on exactly this.

Would you be open to a quick 30-minute call with him to see if there's a fit?
No pitch — he'll just map out what the solution would look like for your situation."
```

## Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "We already use AI tools" | "Good — most businesses have tools but not a system. That's usually where the gap is. Worth 30 minutes to see if there's something being missed?" |
| "We don't have budget" | "I hear you. How much is the current problem costing you? If it's more than a solution would cost, it might be worth the conversation." |
| "Send me an email" | "Happy to. What's the best email? And just so I know what to include — what's the main thing you're dealing with right now?" |
| "Not interested" | "Totally fair. Is it a timing thing or just not relevant to where you're at?" |
| "Who are you again?" | "Arise Group — we build AI automation systems for service businesses. Mekaiel's the founder, he's worked with companies like [Plotter Mechanix] to go from [before] to [after]." |

## What Makes a Qualified Lead

Book the discovery call if they hit **3 of 4**:

- [ ] They described a real, specific problem (not "everything's fine")
- [ ] Problem costs $5K+/month (time, revenue, or staff hours)
- [ ] Problem has been ongoing 3+ months
- [ ] They expressed some interest in fixing it
