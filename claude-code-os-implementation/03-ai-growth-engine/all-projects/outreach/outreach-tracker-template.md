## ICP Scoring Quick Reference

Score leads before outreach:

**Auto-3 (Hot — priority):**
- Pain signal found on LinkedIn/website
- Right size AND right industry
- Decision maker confirmed
- Warm connection or referral

**Auto-2 (Warm — normal priority):**
- Fits ICP on paper
- No pain signal but reachable
- Title match, company size match

**Auto-1 (Cold — low priority):**
- Fits some ICP criteria
- No connection, no signal
- Work through after 3s and 2s exhausted

## Conversion Benchmarks

| Funnel Stage | Benchmark |
|-------------|-----------|
| Email open rate | 40%+ |
| Email reply rate | 8-12% |
| Call connect rate | 15-20% |
| Q1-Q5 completion | 30% of conversations |
| Call booked rate | 1-2 per 10 conversations |
| Discovery → Proposal | 50%+ |
| Proposal → Close | 25-30% |

**Revenue math:**
- 300 dials/week → 50 conversations → 10 Q1-Q5s → 3 calls booked
- 3 calls/week × 4 weeks = 12 discovery calls/month
- 50% → proposal = 6 proposals
- 30% close = ~2 closes/month
- At $5K avg → $10K/month from cold calls alone
