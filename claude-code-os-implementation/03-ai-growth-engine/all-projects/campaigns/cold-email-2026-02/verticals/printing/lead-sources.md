## Lead Qualification Criteria

Before adding a lead to the outreach list, verify:

- [ ] Company size: 2-20 employees
- [ ] Services large format / wide format printers
- [ ] Owner-operator business model (owner is accessible)
- [ ] Has a website or online presence (needed for personalization)
- [ ] Located in US (national geography)
- [ ] Email address found or findable
