# Sequence D: "THE DISAPPEARING INFORMATION" Hook

## Campaign Type
Operations-Focused / Service Manager & Owner Focus

## Target Profile
Operations managers and owners at wide-format printer service companies who experience the frustration of information vanishing between customer interactions - where a second call about the same job requires starting from scratch, where technicians arrive on-site without context, and where "let me look into that" becomes the default response because nobody knows what happened on the last call.

## Emotional Anchor
**Verbatim Quote:** "The second phone call I make to them... nobody else knows what's happening. It just disappears."
**Dream Outcome:** Nothing falls through the cracks - every call auto-logged, AI-summarized, and synced to CRM

Now? Every call is auto-logged. AI creates instant summaries. Everything syncs to their job management system before the phone is back on the hook.

Second call from a customer? The person answering sees the full history. Tech heading to a job? Context is already there.

Here's how we made that happen:

**1. CONSULTING - We found where information was dying**
Interviewed the owner, office manager, and technicians separately. Mapped every handoff point. Identified exactly where context was getting lost - and quantified how many hours it was costing weekly. Delivered a clear operational blueprint before touching any technology.

**2. DEVELOPMENT - We built systems that remember**
Unified phone system that captures every call. Smart routing so the right person answers. AI transcription and summaries generated automatically. Direct integration with their CRM so nothing requires manual entry.

**3. EDUCATION - We trained the team to trust it**
Live workshops to show the new workflow. Daily support during rollout. Video training for every process. 30 days of real human help when questions come up.
