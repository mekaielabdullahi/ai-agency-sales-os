# Sequence C: "THE 50% EMAIL SINKHOLE" Hook

## Campaign Type
Admin-Targeted / Office Manager Focus

## Target Profile
Office managers and administrative staff at wide-format printer service companies who spend the majority of their day managing inbox chaos - sorting service requests, chasing technicians for updates, responding to customer status inquiries, and manually transferring information between email and their job management system.

## Emotional Anchor
**Verbatim Quote:** "If I do 8 hours... maybe 4-5 hours is just looking at emails."
**Dream Outcome:** Half your day back - from 25 hours/week on communications processing down to 10 hours/week

---

```

Here's the approach we used:

**1. CONSULTING - We diagnosed before we prescribed**
We didn't assume we knew the problem. We interviewed the owner, admin, and techs separately. Mapped every touchpoint. Quantified exactly where hours were disappearing. Then delivered a clear blueprint.

Unified phone system with smart routing. Every call auto-logged with AI summaries. Information flows directly into their job management system. No more copy-paste. No more "let me check on that."

Live workshops. Daily support. Video training for every new process. Real humans available when questions come up.
```
