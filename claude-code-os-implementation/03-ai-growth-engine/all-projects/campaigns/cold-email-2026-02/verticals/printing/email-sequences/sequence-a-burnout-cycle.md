# Sequence A: "The Burnout Cycle" Hook

## Campaign Type
Fear-Based (Growth Punishment Pattern)

## Target Profile
Wide-format printer service owners who have experienced the painful cycle: they get ambitious, generate revenue, then get crushed by operational chaos. These owners have learned to fear their own success because every growth spike leads to burnout, depression, and a crash. They've unconsciously started self-sabotaging to avoid the pain of "winning."

## Emotional Anchor
**Verbatim Quote:** "Every time I get ambitious, I start generating revenue, and then I get crushed by it and then I get depressed... stressed..."
**Dream Outcome:** Growth without the crash - systems that scale with revenue instead of breaking under it

**Subject:** `How we broke the burnout cycle in Phoenix`

```
When we worked with Kelsey at Plotter Mechanix, we didn't start with software. We started by asking one question:

"Where exactly do your systems break when revenue goes up?"

Our 3-pillar approach:

**CONSULTING:** We interviewed him, his admin, and his lead tech separately. Mapped every touchpoint. Found the exact moments where growth created chaos instead of profit.

**EDUCATION:** Daily support, training videos, SOPs. Because new systems only work if people actually use them.
```
