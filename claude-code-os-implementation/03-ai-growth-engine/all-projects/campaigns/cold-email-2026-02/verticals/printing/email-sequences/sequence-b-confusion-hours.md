# Sequence B: "The Confusion Hours" Hook

## Campaign Type
Time-Based (Productivity Gap Pattern)

## Target Profile
Wide-format printer service owners who are exhausted but can't point to what they actually accomplished. They work massive hours but feel like they're running in place. The gap between hours worked and hours productive haunts them - they know something is wrong but can't identify what's eating their time.

## Emotional Anchor
**Verbatim Quote:** "I'm working 70 hours a week. But I'm only productive for 40. I'm confused for the other 30."
**Dream Outcome:** Convert chaos hours into revenue hours - reclaim 30 "confused" hours as billable, strategic, or personal time
