## Lead Qualification Criteria

Before adding a lead to the outreach list, verify:

- [ ] Company size: 1-10 employees
- [ ] Operating a real business (not just a hobby blog)
- [ ] Revenue indicators: selling products/courses, 1K+ Etsy sales, or significant following
- [ ] Owner is accessible (email findable, active online)
- [ ] Has a website or online shop presence (needed for personalization)
- [ ] Email address found or findable
