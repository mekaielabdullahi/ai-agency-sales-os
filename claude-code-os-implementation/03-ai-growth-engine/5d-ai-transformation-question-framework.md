## üîç PHASE 1: DISCOVER
**Purpose**: Uncover current state, pain points, and AI readiness
**Question Focus**: What exists today? What's broken? What's manual?

### DISCOVER Question Framework:

#### Business Operations Layer:
**Template**: "How do you currently [PROCESS]? What breaks? What's manual?"

**Core Discovery Questions**:
1. **Current State Mapping**
   - "Walk me through how [OPERATION] works today from start to finish."
   - "What systems/tools are you using for [FUNCTION]?"
   - "How many people touch [PROCESS] before it's complete?"

2. **Pain Point Identification**
   - "Where does [PROCESS] typically slow down or break?"
   - "What takes the most time in [OPERATION]?"
   - "What are you doing manually that you wish was automated?"

3. **Data & System Assessment**
   - "What data do you collect during [PROCESS]?"
   - "How do you currently track/measure [METRIC]?"
   - "Do your systems talk to each other, or is there manual handoff?"

4. **AI Readiness Check**
   - "Have you tried automation or AI in [AREA] before?"
   - "What's preventing you from automating [PROCESS] now?"
   - "Do you have historical data for [FUNCTION]?"

---

## üé® PHASE 2: DESIGN
**Purpose**: Envision ideal future state with AI transformation
**Question Focus**: What should it look like? What outcomes do you want?

### DESIGN Question Framework:

#### Vision & Requirements:
**Template**: "If we could transform [PROCESS] with AI, what would success look like?"

**Core Design Questions**:
1. **Ideal State Definition**
   - "If you could wave a magic wand, how would [OPERATION] work perfectly?"
   - "What would you want AI to handle vs what humans should still do?"
   - "Describe your perfect [PROCESS] - what happens automatically?"

2. **Outcome Specification**
   - "What specific results would make this transformation successful?"
   - "How would you measure success - time saved, cost reduced, revenue increased?"
   - "What KPIs would you track to know AI is working?"

3. **User Experience Design**
   - "Who will interact with this AI system and how?"
   - "What information do [USERS] need instant access to?"
   - "How should AI communicate results - dashboard, alerts, reports?"

4. **Integration Requirements**
   - "What systems must the AI solution integrate with?"
   - "What data needs to flow between systems automatically?"
   - "Who needs visibility into what parts of the process?"

5. **Constraints & Boundaries**
   - "What must NOT change during transformation?"
   - "What regulatory/compliance requirements apply?"
   - "What's your risk tolerance for AI making decisions autonomously?"

---

## üõ†Ô∏è PHASE 3: DEVELOP
**Purpose**: Understand technical requirements and build approach
**Question Focus**: What do we need to build? What data/access/resources?

### DEVELOP Question Framework:

#### Technical Discovery:
**Template**: "What do we need access to in order to build [SOLUTION]?"

**Core Development Questions**:
1. **Data Access & Quality**
   - "Where is the data for [PROCESS] stored currently?"
   - "Can we access that data via API, export, or direct connection?"
   - "How clean/consistent is the data? Any known quality issues?"
   - "How far back does historical data go?"

2. **System Access & Permissions**
   - "Who can grant us access to [SYSTEM/DATA]?"
   - "Are there IT security reviews required before integration?"
   - "What's your typical vendor onboarding/access process?"

3. **Technical Environment**
   - "What infrastructure do you prefer - cloud, on-premise, hybrid?"
   - "Any technical constraints we should know about (security, compliance, legacy systems)?"
   - "Who will be our technical point of contact?"

4. **Testing & Validation Requirements**
   - "How will we test the AI solution before full rollout?"
   - "Who needs to validate accuracy before we go live?"
   - "What's your acceptable error rate for [AI DECISION TYPE]?"

5. **Build Timeline & Phasing**
   - "Are there phases/priorities - what must be built first?"
   - "Are there seasonal factors affecting when we can deploy?"
   - "What's the minimum viable solution that would provide value?"

---

## üöÄ PHASE 4: DEPLOY
**Purpose**: Understand rollout strategy and change management
**Question Focus**: How do we implement? Who needs training? How do we transition?

### DEPLOY Question Framework:

#### Implementation Planning:
**Template**: "How do we roll this out without disrupting [OPERATIONS]?"

**Core Deployment Questions**:
1. **Rollout Strategy**
   - "Do you prefer phased rollout or big-bang deployment?"
   - "Can we pilot with one team/location/process first?"
   - "What's the fallback plan if something doesn't work initially?"

2. **Change Management**
   - "Who will be affected by this AI transformation?"
   - "How do you typically introduce new systems/processes to your team?"
   - "Who are the early adopters vs skeptics we should plan for?"

3. **Training & Adoption**
   - "What level of training will your team need?"
   - "Who will be the internal champion/owner of the AI system?"
   - "How will you ensure adoption vs people reverting to old ways?"

4. **Transition Planning**
   - "What's the cutover plan from old process to AI-enabled process?"
   - "How long will you run old and new systems in parallel?"
   - "What happens to legacy data/systems after deployment?"

5. **Success Criteria for Launch**
   - "What needs to happen in the first 30 days for you to consider this successful?"
   - "Who signs off that deployment was successful?"
   - "What metrics will you track immediately post-launch?"

---

## üìà PHASE 5: DRIVE
**Purpose**: Ensure ongoing optimization and value realization
**Question Focus**: How do we measure? How do we improve? How do we scale?

### DRIVE Question Framework:

#### Optimization & Scaling:
**Template**: "How do we ensure continuous improvement and expanding value?"

**Core Drive Questions**:
1. **Performance Monitoring**
   - "How often do you want to review AI system performance?"
   - "What dashboard/reporting would help you track ROI?"
   - "Who needs to see what metrics regularly?"

2. **Continuous Improvement**
   - "How will we identify opportunities to improve the AI over time?"
   - "Who will provide feedback on what's working vs what's not?"
   - "What's the process for requesting enhancements or adjustments?"

3. **Value Realization**
   - "How will you track ROI - time saved, cost reduced, revenue impact?"
   - "What would justify expanding AI to other areas of the business?"
   - "When would you consider this AI investment 'paid off'?"

4. **Scaling Strategy**
   - "Once this works, what other processes would benefit from AI?"
   - "Would you want to expand to other departments/locations?"
   - "What would trigger the decision to scale vs stay at current scope?"

5. **Long-term Partnership**
   - "What ongoing support do you expect after deployment?"
   - "How involved do you want us in monitoring and optimization?"
   - "Should we plan quarterly reviews to identify new AI opportunities?"

## üéØ QUESTION GENERATION PROCESS

### Step-by-Step System:

**STEP 1: Identify Client's Business Model**
- What do they sell?
- How do they deliver value?
- What's their revenue model?

**STEP 2: Map Critical Business Operations**
- What functions exist? (Sales, Ops, Marketing, Service, Finance, HR)
- Which are most critical to revenue/delivery?
- Which have the most pain?

**STEP 3: Select 5D Phase for Conversation**
- First call = DISCOVER
- Discovery call = DISCOVER + DESIGN
- Proposal stage = DEVELOP + DEPLOY
- Post-sale = DEPLOY + DRIVE

**STEP 4: Generate Questions Using Matrix**
- Pick business operation layer (e.g., Sales)
- Pick 5D phase (e.g., Discover)
- Apply question templates from framework
- Customize with client-specific context

**STEP 5: Prioritize by Impact**
- Which operations have biggest pain?
- Which operations have biggest ROI potential?
- Which operations are easiest AI wins?

## üí° POSITIONING AS AI TRANSFORMATION PARTNER

### How Questions Position You:

**DISCOVER Questions** ‚Üí Show you understand their business deeply
**DESIGN Questions** ‚Üí Position you as strategic advisor, not vendor
**DEVELOP Questions** ‚Üí Demonstrate technical expertise
**DEPLOY Questions** ‚Üí Show you care about adoption, not just delivery
**DRIVE Questions** ‚Üí Position long-term partnership, not project

### Language Patterns:

**Don't say**: "We build AI solutions"
**Do say**: "We transform business operations through systematic AI adoption"

**Don't say**: "What do you want us to build?"
**Do say**: "Let's discover where AI can unlock the most value in your operations"

**Don't say**: "This will be done in 6 weeks"
**Do say**: "We'll develop the solution, deploy it with your team, and drive continuous improvement"
