## 9. RISKS & MITIGATION

### Risk #1: Premium pricing scares away small businesses
**Mitigation:**
- That's the point—we want B2B firms with budget
- Tier 3 (Audit) is accessible entry point ($5K-$7.5K)
- Frame as investment with clear ROI, not expense

### Risk #2: Tier 3 doesn't convert to Tier 2/1
**Mitigation:**
- Make audit deliverable so valuable they want to execute
- Build Tier 2/1 pricing into audit recommendations
- Credit Tier 3 fee toward Tier 2/1 engagement
- Follow up 2 weeks after audit presentation

### Risk #3: Can't deliver on fast SLAs
**Mitigation:**
- Limit Tier 1 partnerships to 3-5 max
- Use AriseCore tech assets to speed delivery
- Hire additional engineers if needed
- Under-promise, over-deliver on timelines

### Risk #4: Market doesn't understand "embedded partnership"
**Mitigation:**
- Use analogies: "Like having an in-house AI team without hiring"
- Show case studies of Tier 1 partnerships
- Lead with business outcomes, explain model after
