## Email Frameworks

### AIDA (Attention-Interest-Desire-Action)
1. **Attention**: Hook in subject/opening
2. **Interest**: Relevance to reader
3. **Desire**: Benefits and outcomes
4. **Action**: Clear CTA

### PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution)
1. **Problem**: Identify the pain
2. **Agitate**: Make it feel urgent
3. **Solution**: Present your answer

### 4P (Promise-Picture-Proof-Push)
1. **Promise**: What they'll get
2. **Picture**: Paint the outcome
3. **Proof**: Evidence it works
4. **Push**: CTA with urgency

### Story Framework
1. Hook with story opening
2. Build tension/challenge
3. Resolution/transformation
4. Relate to reader
5. CTA

## Subject Line Formulas

- **Curiosity**: "The [adjective] truth about [topic]"
- **Benefit**: "How to [achieve outcome] in [timeframe]"
- **Urgency**: "[X] hours left: [offer]"
- **Personal**: "Quick question, [Name]"
- **Numbers**: "[X] ways to [benefit]"
