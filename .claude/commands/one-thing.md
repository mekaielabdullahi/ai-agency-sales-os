## Your Task

Help identify THE ONE THING through strategic questioning.

## Discovery Process

### Step 1: Clarify the Goal
Ask: "What's your primary objective right now?"
- Current OBG (Overarching Business Goal)
- This week's main target
- Today's most important outcome

### Step 2: Apply the Focusing Question

For the BIG picture:
"What's the ONE THING I can do **someday** such that by doing it everything else will be easier or unnecessary?"

For the SMALL focus:
"What's the ONE THING I can do **right now** such that by doing it everything else will be easier or unnecessary?"

### Step 3: Validate the Answer

Check if the ONE THING:
- [ ] Directly advances the OBG
- [ ] Creates leverage (enables other things)
- [ ] Is specific and actionable
- [ ] Can be started immediately
- [ ] Has a clear "done" state

### Step 4: Remove Obstacles

Identify what could prevent THE ONE THING:
- Time blockers
- Dependency issues
- Energy/motivation
- Environmental factors
