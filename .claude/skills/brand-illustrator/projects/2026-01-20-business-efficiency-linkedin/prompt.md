- Use the signature gradient (amber → gold → soft teal) to illuminate the transformation zone and the streamlined side

- The right "after" side should glow with the full Arise gradient

Focal Point: The transformation zone in the center where complexity becomes clarity, illuminated with amber-gold gradient glow.

Supporting Elements:

Composition: Transformation View - clear left-to-right visual flow showing improvement. The transformation zone is highlighted with the Arise Glow gradient. Include subtle directional cues guiding the eye through the transformation. 60% transformation/after zone, 30% before zone, 10% breathing room.

Format: 1080 x 1080 pixels, square aspect ratio for LinkedIn post.

Quality: High resolution, crisp details, clean edges on all elements, consistent lighting throughout, professional polished finish.

Do NOT include: cold blue colors, pure white or pure black, harsh shadows, photorealistic elements, stock photo aesthetic, generic tech imagery, threatening imagery, cluttered or chaotic compositions, cartoon style, rough textures, dark atmosphere, cool gray tones.
