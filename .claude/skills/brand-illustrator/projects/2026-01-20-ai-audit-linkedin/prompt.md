# AI Audit Discovery - LinkedIn Post Illustration

## Generation Prompt

Create a warm tech illustration in isometric perspective showing a large, detailed magnifying glass examining a stylized business system diagram. The magnifying glass has a warm amber lens that reveals glowing insights and organized automation opportunities within.

**Style**: Sophisticated and detailed but human and approachable - like Linear meets Stripe meets Notion but warmer. The "Warm Tech" aesthetic combines technical credibility with human warmth. Precise, clean line work (not sketchy). Soft, diffused shadows (never harsh). Detailed but not cluttered. Premium digital illustration quality.

**Focal Point**: The magnifying glass with its amber-tinted lens. Inside the lens, the system diagram appears clearer and brighter, with discovered opportunities highlighted as glowing nodes in the amber-gold gradient.

**Supporting Elements**:
- Organized flowchart/system diagram beneath the magnifying glass
- Data points and connection lines
- Nodes that glow with the Arise gradient where insights are found
- Subtle floating particles representing data/analysis
- Soft ambient glow emanating from the discoveries

**Colors**:
- Primary accent: Warm amber (#E8914F) and gold (#F5C26B) for the lens glow and discoveries
- Secondary accent: Soft teal (#2D7D8A) for depth and some connection lines
- Background: Soft cream (#FDF8F3) with warm white fade
- Structure/outlines: Deep navy (#1A2B4A) for the magnifying glass frame and diagram lines
- Use the signature gradient (amber → gold → soft teal) for the highlighted discoveries inside the lens
- Warm, sunrise-like color temperature throughout

**Composition**: Focus View - centered composition with the magnifying glass as the clear focal point. The system diagram is visible beneath and through the lens, showing the "before" (standard diagram) transforming into "after" (glowing insights) through the lens. Clean negative space around edges. Subject occupies about 60-70% of frame.

**Format**: 1080 x 1080 pixels, square aspect ratio for LinkedIn.

**Quality**:
- High resolution, crisp details
- No artifacts or noise
- Clean edges on all elements
- Consistent warm lighting from top-left
- Professional, polished finish
- Ready for commercial use

**Do NOT include**:
- Cold blue colors
- Pure white or pure black
- Harsh shadows
- Photorealistic elements
- Stock photo aesthetic
- Generic tech imagery (random circuits, generic globes)
- Threatening or intimidating representations
- Cluttered or chaotic composition
- Cartoonish or childish style
- Rough textures or sketchy lines
- Dark or ominous atmosphere
- Cool gray tones
