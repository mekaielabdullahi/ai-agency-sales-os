# Editor Context: Video 1 (45-Second Testimonial)

Simple guide for video editor. Everything you need to edit this video.

## Quick Facts

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| How long? | 45 seconds |
| What is it? | A happy customer talking about our work |
| Where will it go? | Website, LinkedIn, Instagram |
| What should viewers feel? | "I trust these people" |

## Who is Kelsey?

Kelsey owns a small company in Phoenix, Arizona (USA).
His company is called **Plotter Mechanix**.
He fixes large printers and plotters for businesses.

**Before our help:**
- He was very stressed
- His phone never stopped ringing
- Information was getting lost
- He worked 70+ hours every week

**After our help:**
- His phone calls are automatically recorded
- Information goes straight to his team
- He saves many hours every week
- He is happy and trusts the system

```
BEGINNING: Kelsey had problems. Too much work. No time.
    ↓
MIDDLE: AriseGroup built a phone system that helps him.
    ↓
END: Kelsey is happy. The system works. He recommends us.
```

## The 3 Quotes to Use

### Quote 1 (0:05-0:15) - THE HOOK
**"I could see how we could double our income."**

- **What it means:** Kelsey now believes his business can grow 2x bigger
- **Emotion:** HOPEFUL, excited, seeing the future
- **How he should look:** Eyes wide, energetic, genuine belief
- **Why this works:** Big statement that grabs attention immediately

**Source:** Jan 27 Operations Update transcript

---

### Quote 2 (0:15-0:25) - THE FEELING
**"I'm so excited about what's going on. I feel like I had this feeling a long time ago..."**

- **What it means:** Kelsey feels like a new business owner again. He has energy and hope.
- **Emotion:** JOY, nostalgia, excitement
- **How he should look:** Smiling, emotional, genuine
- **Why this works:** Shows personal transformation, not just business numbers

**Source:** Jan 27 Operations Update transcript

---

### Quote 3 (0:35-0:42) - THE ENDORSEMENT
**"I'm gonna be your commercial, dude."**

- **What it means:** Kelsey is so happy, he will tell everyone about us
- **Emotion:** GRATEFUL, endorsing, genuine
- **How he should look:** Smiling big, leaning in, authentic moment
- **Why this works:** Direct recommendation to viewers

**Source:** Jan 27 Operations Update transcript

## Graphics Needed

### Lower Third (Name Bar)

Show at 0:05 when Kelsey first appears:

```
┌────────────────────────────┐
│  KELSEY                    │
│  Owner, Plotter Mechanix   │
└────────────────────────────┘
```

- Position: Bottom left of screen
- Colors: Use brand colors (navy background, white text)
- Animation: Fade in, stay 5 seconds, fade out

---

### Big Numbers (0:25-0:35)

Show these numbers in the center of screen:

```
$5K → $73K Annual Value
1,378% ROI
```

**What these numbers mean:**
- $5K = What Kelsey paid us ($5,000)
- $73K = How much value he gets each year ($73,000)
- 1,378% ROI = Return on Investment (very high number, very good)

**Style:**
- Big, bold text
- White with shadow so it is easy to read
- Can animate numbers counting up (optional)

## What This Video Should Feel Like

**Mood:** Honest, real, not too polished

**Think of it like:** A friend recommending a restaurant
- Not scripted
- Not too perfect
- Just real person, real results

**Music:** NO background music (or very very quiet)
- We want it to feel authentic
- Raw and real

## Export Versions Needed

You need to make 4 different versions:

| Version | Size | Where It Goes |
|---------|------|---------------|
| Wide (16:9) | 1920x1080 | Website |
| Square (1:1) | 1080x1080 | LinkedIn |
| Vertical (9:16) | 1080x1920 | Instagram, TikTok |
| Wide with audio | 1920x1080 | Testimonials page |

**Important for Square/Vertical:**
- Keep Kelsey's face in center
- Move text overlays so they fit

---

## Captions

**ALL versions need captions burned in** (text on video, not separate file)

Why? Many people watch videos with sound off.

Caption style:
- Big enough to read on phone
- White text with dark shadow
- Bottom center of screen
