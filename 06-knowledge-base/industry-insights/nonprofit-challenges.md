# Nonprofit Operational Challenges & AI Opportunities

**Target**: Nonprofit organizations (10-100 staff)

---

## Top Pain Points

### 1. Donor Management Chaos
- Donor data scattered across systems
- Manual thank-you letters
- Hard to segment donors
- **AI Solution**: Automated donor segmentation, personalized communication

### 2. Grant Reporting Burden
- Each funder wants different format
- 10-20 hours per report
- **AI Solution**: Auto-generated grant reports, data aggregation

### 3. Volunteer Coordination
- Scheduling nightmare
- Skill matching manual
- **AI Solution**: Volunteer matching AI, automated scheduling

### 4. Impact Measurement
- Data collection inconsistent
- Hard to quantify outcomes
- **AI Solution**: Impact dashboards, automated reporting

---

## Messaging for Nonprofits

**Lead With Mission**:
✅ "Free up 10 hours/week to serve more families"
❌ "Increase efficiency"

**Address Budget**:
"Cost recovery in 6 months through efficiency, then ongoing savings fund more programs"
